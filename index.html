Tips

  • If you're on mobile press the start button and then zoom out to see the whole image.
  • The sequence input is case insensitive.
  • You can right-click the image to "Save image as...". The white background is transparent when you save!
  • You can click on the color-boxes in the settings section to change them! After that, redraw the image with the start-button.
  • If your image output is too big, just resize the canvas in the settings and click the start-button again to redraw.
